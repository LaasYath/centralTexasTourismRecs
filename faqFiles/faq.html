Frequently Asked Questions

Need some help? Check out our FAQ. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to send us a message on our contact page!

What's so special about Central Texas?

Central Texas is the home of great culture and history. The "Keep Austin Weird" movement has perserved the authentic Texan culture. You'll come across beautiful parks and rivers, perfect for fishing, hiking, kayaking, swimming, and so much more! Pick flowers or fruit from family owned farms, visit rodeos, sunflower festivals, music parades, and other local events all year round! Don't miss out, and come visit today!

Yes! You can find a list of family-friendly activities by using the search tool on our attractions page!

What is the weather like?

Prepare for hot summers, with highs of over 100 degrees farenheit! Remember to pack your sunscreen! Enjoy perfect spring and fall weather with averages of high 60s and low 70s.

What does the city life look like?

Austin is abuzz with restaurants, music, and bars. 6th Street is a common go-to for many residents!

Are there any lakes or beaches?

While Central Texas ia bit far from the Gulf of Mexico, we do have many lakes for visitors to go swimming, kayaking, fishing, and so much more!
